HOW TO PLAY

The Game

A fast arena ball game. Steel walls keep the ball in play — no out-of-bounds, no fouls. Throw the ball into the glowing goal bay at the far end to score 10 points. Two halves; most points wins.

On the Pitch

You control the highlighted player; your team-mates and opponents are AI. Control switches to whichever team-mate catches the ball. Tackle the carrier to jar the ball loose — big hits can send a player to the medics. Knocked-down players get up after a moment.

The League

Seven match days against the other seven teams. Results earn credits — 30 a win, 15 a draw, 5 a loss, plus 2 per goal — which you spend in the team screen (+1 stat costs 10). The other fixtures are simulated each match day.

Match Tools

AUTO COACH lets the AI play your team while you take a break. The SPEED dial runs the match from 0.25× slow motion to 4× fast forward. ARENA changes the scenery; SOUND toggles the effects.

Controls (click a key to change it — ESC cancels)

The arrow keys always steer, whatever else you bind.

On phones and tablets a joystick and action buttons appear on screen during matches — no keyboard needed.
